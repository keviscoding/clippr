We guarantee fast, reliable payouts — but to protect the app's margins, every single rule below must be met before payment is released. Missing one rule means missing the payout. We don't negotiate this.

01

Mandatory App Reveal & CTA

A video with 10 million views means nothing if viewers don't know what our app is. Every video must clearly show the app being used and have a clear call-to-action — e.g. "Link in bio to download."

RULE: No App = No Pay.
02

The Tier-1 Geography Rule

We're promoting an English-speaking app. When submitting for payout, attach a screenshot of your native analytics (Audience Demographics). At least 50% of views must come from Tier-1 countries — USA, UK, Canada, Australia, etc.

If your video goes viral in non-English regions, it won't convert for us, and we can't pay it out.

03

The 5 Million View Cutoff (Cap)

Payouts max out at 5 million views per video — a maximum of $1,250 per single video.

Why? Past 5M, the algorithms push videos to global audiences (non-English speakers, young children) who don't download the app.

04

The 7-Day Maturation Rule

You can only submit a video for payout 7 days after it was posted. This lets the video finish accumulating its viral views so we can pay you accurately in one lump sum.

05

Zero Tolerance for Fake Engagement

Do not buy views. Do not use engagement pods, view-bots, or boosting groups. We actively monitor watch-time graphs in your analytics. Botted views have a 0.01-second average duration — they're obvious.

CONSEQUENCE: Permanent ban + forfeit of all unpaid earnings. Immediately.
